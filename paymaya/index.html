Frequently Asked Questions

Is Maya really a bank?

Yes—Maya Bank, Inc. is a BSP-licensed digital bank and deposits are insured by PDIC up to ₱500 k.

Who can open an account?

Philippine residents aged 18+ with one valid government ID.

How do I cash-in?

Via InstaPay, PESONet, over-the-counter partners, or debit/credit card.

Are there fees?

Savings and transfers to other Maya users are free. InstaPay/PESONet fees apply for some banks.
